David Pecker is expected to be the first witness called in Trump hush money trial, source says

David Pecker by Marion Curtis

Pecker, former chairman of National Enquirer's parent company, is set to testify in the Manhattan DA's trial on hush money related to Trump. He's central to the alleged scheme involving Stormy Daniels. The $130,000 payment, a key point in the case against Trump, was reimbursed to Cohen by the Trump Organization. Daniels and Cohen are expected witnesses. Pecker has immunity, and AMI has a non-prosecution agreement.

Spice Girls Reunite at Victoria Beckham's 50th Birthday

The Spice Girls reunited for Posh's 50th birthday, as David Beckham shared on Instagram. They sang Stop with David joining in. Victoria and Mel B teased future plans, while Emma and Mel C posed together. This marks their first official gathering since 2013.

Meg Bennett, 'Young and the Restless' and 'General Hospital' Actress and Writer, Dead at 75

Meg Bennett by CBS

Meg Bennett, a beloved TV writer and actress known for her work on soap operas like The Bold and the Beautiful and General Hospital, died at 75 on April 11 after battling cancer. She leaves behind her husband, two stepdaughters, and four grandchildren.
